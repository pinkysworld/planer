# Modern Planer

A modern trucking business simulation game inspired by the classic "Der Planer" games from Greenwood Entertainment (1994-1996). Build your transport empire, manage your fleet, and balance work with family life!

## Features

### Business Simulation
- **Contract Management**: Accept delivery contracts from various clients across Europe
- **Fleet Management**: Purchase, maintain, and sell trucks (diesel, electric, hydrogen)
- **Employee Management**: Hire and manage drivers, mechanics, accountants, and more
- **Route Planning**: Plan optimal routes between cities for maximum profit
- **Station Expansion**: Open stations in new cities across Central Europe, Scandinavia, and Southern Europe
- **Financial Management**: Take loans, manage expenses, and grow your company

### Modern Updates (vs Original)
- Email communication instead of fax
- Modern truck models with electric and hydrogen options
- Current fuel prices and regulations
- Updated graphics while maintaining the classic pixel art style
- GPS and route optimization technology

### Private Life Simulation
- Balance work with family life
- Buy luxury items to increase social status
- Keep your family happy
- Work towards retirement

### Game Modes
- **Freeplay**: Build your empire without restrictions
- **Startup Challenge**: Start with limited funds and reach â‚¬500,000
- **European Expansion**: Open stations in 5 different countries
- **Fleet Commander**: Build a fleet of 20 trucks
- **Reputation Master**: Achieve 95% reputation rating

### Office Building
Navigate a multi-floor office building with different rooms:
- **Basement**: Garage, PR & Marketing, Truck Dealer, Exit/Travel
- **Floor 1**: Reception, Contracts, Dispatch, Station Management
- **Floor 2**: Your Office, Accounting, HR, Email/Communications, Bank
- **Floor 3**: Board Room, Statistics, Luxury Shop, Go Home

## Controls

- **A/D or Arrow Keys**: Move left/right
- **E or Enter**: Interact with rooms/doors
- **ESC**: Pause menu
- **Mouse**: Click on rooms to enter

## System Requirements

- **OS**: macOS 10.15+, Windows 10+, Linux
- **Engine**: Godot 4.2+
- **Resolution**: 1280x720 minimum

## Building from Source

1. Install [Godot Engine 4.2+](https://godotengine.org/download)
2. Clone this repository
3. Open `project.godot` in Godot
4. Export for your target platform

### Steam Integration

The game is designed to be compatible with Steam. To add Steam integration:
1. Enable the GodotSteam plugin
2. Configure your Steam App ID
3. Set up achievements and cloud saves
